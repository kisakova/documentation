**Next steps**

You can perform the following actions with every item in the Customers list:
   
  * `View a Customer details <view>`_: Click on the item to open its details page.
   
   OR
   
   Aternatively: Hover over the |IcMore| *more actions* menu to the right of the item and click the |IcView| to open its details page.

 * `Edit a Customer details <edit>`_: Hover over the |IcMore| *more actions* menu to the right of the item and click the |IcEdit| to start editing its details.
